# Behavioral Interview Questions

## Intro

These questions help engineers prepare concise, evidence-based stories for senior-level interviews.

## Communication And Ownership

### Question

How do you answer "Tell me about a challenging production incident"?

### Short Answer

Describe the context, the technical issue, your role, how you diagnosed it, what you changed, and what the team learned afterward.

### Deep Explanation

The answer should show technical judgment under pressure, clear communication, and concrete follow-through rather than a vague crisis story.

### Senior-Level Notes

Strong candidates include prevention steps, not just firefighting.

### Common Mistakes

- telling a story with no measurable outcome

### Question

How do you talk about disagreement with another engineer?

### Short Answer

Frame it around trade-offs, constraints, and how you reached a better decision or resolution rather than around personalities.

### Deep Explanation

Senior interviewers want to see maturity in handling technical disagreement, not just confidence in your own opinion.

### Senior-Level Notes

Strong answers show listening, evidence, and collaboration.

### Common Mistakes

- turning the story into blame
